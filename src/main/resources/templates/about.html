Basic Info :

Hateya H.h.u.high School is an academic institute located at Hateya Sakhipur Tangail. Its institute code (EIIN) is 114596 . It was established in 01 Jan, 1964. Its co-education type is Combined. The institute has following 3 disciplines : Science, Business Studies, Humanities. Average exam passing rate of the institute is : JSC/Equivalent: 83.68%,SSC/Equivalent: 83.68%, Its MPO number is 4210061301. It has Day shift. Its management is Managing.
